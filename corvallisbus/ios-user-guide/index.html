Corvallis Bus

iOS User Guide

  • Browse bus stops to find when buses will arrive and where they are going.
  • Add your frequently used stops to favorites and view them in the Favorites tab.
  • Check the arrival times for your favorite stops quickly by adding the Corvallis Bus widget to the Today view.
  • Make sure service is operating normally by checking the Service Alerts tab.
  • Customize app behaviors and learn more about how Corvallis Bus works in the Preferences tab.
